Bakakeng Norte is one of the places in Baguio where a lot of home owners live. Like other people citizens in these community also own pets, like dogs, cats, and other kind of animals. It is the responsibility of the pet owners to know and be aware about animal welfare.

What is Animal Welfare?

Animal welfare is all about the well-being of the animals. Giving respect to animal welfare is based on how people believe that animals are also living creatures that also has feelings and that animals also experience suffering and pain. The animal welfare is especially for the animals that are in the care of humans, like the pet owners. Animal owners should always consider the things that will help them take care the animals under their supervision and to make sure that they follow the law regarding animal welfare.
